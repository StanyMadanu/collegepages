RHL collaborates with AU to develop a certificate course

Posted On: 05-09-2022

Digital Solutions for Women & Social Problems" is a course that would focus on identifying social problems and finding a digital solution to overcome them. The course speaks about social problems in-depth, explains the current digital solutions that can be used and how to unite these two worlds to come up with a plausible solution. Andhra University and RHL's collaboration for this course ensures that students get the most out of the course and can become aware of the social, technological and financial aspects of dealing with such circumstances in a broader spectrum.

VC and Registrar laud 'WoW - World of Women' mobile app

Posted On: 05-09-2022

WoW — World of Women", developed by Rural Health Care In, is a health niche mobile application that focuses on providing a safe haven where women can understand their health problems and send queries regarding them. The app has many features including an AI-powered symptom checker, call-to-doctor, period tracker, query recording system, etc. WoW aims, through its technology, to bring medical advice to the large population of women who have been deprived of such essential information. All the information provided in the app is provided by doctors, and users can check or record queries with complete privacy.
